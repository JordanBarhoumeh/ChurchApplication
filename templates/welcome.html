Welcome to Trinity Apps

By Jordan Barhoumeh

This app acts as a landing page for your church, its events, and other exciting features.

To use the app, add it to your homescreen. Then, open it and enter the code provided by your church.

Experience a seamless way to stay connected with your church community, access event schedules, read service books, and even participate in discussions or groups. Don't forget to check out the option to contribute or donate directly through the app, making it easier than ever to support your church's missions and activities.
